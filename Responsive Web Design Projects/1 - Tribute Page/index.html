Big Boss

The Legendary Soldier

Here's a time line of Big Boss’s life:

  • 1935 - Born in United States, exact location is unknown
  • 1950 - Becoming The Boss' disciple in June 1950. Fought in the Korean War
  • 1954 - Was involved in the Bikini Atoll test of the first airborne detonation of a hydrogen bomb. Unlike most of his friends present during the test, Jack did not develop symptoms of leukemia, thyroid cancer, or radiation poisoning, but was rendered sterile
  • 1961 - Carried out covert operations during the early stages of the Vietnam War, before America officially participated
  • 1964 - Served in the Green Berets for several years. By late 1964, he had become experienced at sniping in urban and marine environments, and had eaten snakes during previous survival training exercises. On August 24 took part in the Virtuous Mission as a member of the CIA's FOX Unit, in which he was to rescue defecting Soviet scientist Nikolai Stepanovich Sokolov
  • 1966 - Encountered a war orphan known as Frank Jaeger during the Mozambican War of Independence
  • 1970 - The FOX Unit was considered to be rogue after its members organized a revolt, so he left FOX Unit, dealt with it and then officially formed FOXHOUND. Was invited to join in his formation of a new organization, which would later be known as the Patriots. After that Les Enfants Terribles incident happened
  • 1971 - Participated in a mission to rescue EVA from Hanoi in 1971, who later joined Patriots herself. Found out about Les Enfants Terribles project and lefts country, FOXHOUND and Patriots. Returned to Vietnam as a lone soldier and joined an LRRP, fought alongside SOG, the Green Berets and the Wild Geese, completing more than 70 missions
  • 1972 - Hired by the Colombian government to work with its army. There, Big Boss meets Kazuhira Miller and established his own mercenary group known as Militaires Sans Frontières
  • 1974 - Peace Walker Incident
  • 1975 - Fell into a nine-year coma after Ground Zeroes incident
  • 1984 - Awoke from coma in the Cypriot hospital. Few months later Big Boss made his move and Then Phantom Pain incident happened
  • 1990 - Returned to the U.S. to once again take command of FOXHOUND, the special forces unit he founded two decades earlier. While serving as FOXHOUND commander, he secretly built up his mercenary company Outer Heaven into a larger military establishment
  • 1995 - Solid Snake joined FOXHOUND. Big Boss taught Snake the techniques of CQC and the importance of having the will to survive on the battlefield. As a result Outer Heaven Uprising and Metal Gear incident happened
  • 1997 - Participated in the Mercenary War, giving a fledgling nation on the border of the former USSR, Pakistan, China, and Afghanistan its independence. Zanzibar Province, a former autonomous zone of the USSR, thus became Zanzibar Land with Big Boss as its president. Sometimes after gets defeated by solid snake and burned by flamethrower. Big Boss's body was recovered by the Patriots. Despite suffering major injuries, he was still alive but in a comatose state
  • 2005 - Shadow Moses incident
  • 2014 - The Patriots' AI was destroyed. Thus allowing Big Boss to awaken from his coma. Guns of a Patrions incident happened and leads to his death. A tombstone to commemorate Big Boss had been erected in Arlington National Cemetery. Placed next to The Boss's grave, it read: "A Hero Forever Loyal to the Flames of War, Rests in Outer Heaven”

"We have no nation, no philosophy, no ideology. We go where we're needed, fighting not for country, not for government, but for ourselves. We need no reason to fight. We fight because we are needed. We will be the deterrent for those with no other recourse. We are soldiers without borders, our purpose defined by the era we live in."

-- Jack, a.k.a Naked Snake, a.k.a. Big Boss
